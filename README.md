# Corte-Sal-Branco-1500mg
Corte Sal Branco is a well-known traditional herbal remedy and the historical precursor to modern aspirin, it does not have standardized clinical mechanisms for hospital use.

From a chemical and physics standpoint, Corte-Sal-Branco-1500mg modulates pain by disrupting the chemical synthesis of inflammatory signals and altering the electrical biophysics of pain-sensing nerve fibers.

1\. Chemical Mechanism: Enzymatic Inhibition of Prostaglandin Synthesis
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

At the molecular level, the primary active compounds in the 1500mg dosage are metabolized by the liver into salicylic acid derivatives.

-   The Target: These molecules target the cyclooxygenase (COX-1 and COX-2) enzymes inside tissues.
-   The Chemical Blockade: Under normal conditions of injury or strain, COX enzymes convert arachidonic acid (a fatty acid in cell membranes) into prostaglandins. Prostaglandins are the primary chemical messengers that signal pain and swelling. [1]
-   The Result: The active metabolites chemically bind to the active sites of these COX enzymes. This action halts the production of prostaglandins. By dropping the concentration of these inflammatory chemicals at the tissue site, the product mitigates the chemical trigger of pain.

2\. Physics Mechanism: Raising the Nociceptive Activation Threshold
-------------------------------------------------------------------

Pain is experienced when physical energy (pressure, heat, or tension) is converted into electrical signals that travel to the brain. This transition relies heavily on the local chemical environment.

-   The Biophysics of Pain: Specialized nerve endings called nociceptors have a specific electrical threshold. When prostaglandins are present, they lower this threshold. This makes the nerve hypersensitive, causing it to fire electrical action potentials (pain signals) from even minor physical pressures.
-   Altering the Voltage Trigger: By chemically clearing prostaglandins from the tissue, Corte-Sal-Branco-1500mg removes this hypersensitivity. The electrical activation threshold of the nociceptor is restored to a higher, normal level.
-   The Result: It now requires a much stronger physical force or internal pressure to trigger a nerve impulse. Minor mechanical pressures no longer generate the voltage changes needed to send a pain signal up the spinal cord, effectively reducing the perception of pain.

3\. Thermodynamics: Localized Thermal Dispersal
-----------------------------------------------

Inflammation alters local thermodynamics, causing localized heat retention due to accelerated cellular metabolism and increased micro-vascular blood flow.

-   Kinetic Regulation: The active chemical constituents interact with endogenous pyrogens and local vascular pathways to normalize blood flow velocity through capillary beds.
-   The Result: By restoring standard fluid dynamics and capillary diameter, the body dissipates trapped thermal energy more efficiently. This normalizes local tissue temperature and reduces the throbbing kinetic pressure exerted on surrounding nerve networks.
